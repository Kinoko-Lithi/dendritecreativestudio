About the Artist

"I work with fragmentation, distortion and digital residue. My practice explores layered perception and abstract memory."


Tasos Zisis, Dendrite's founder, is a digital artist and musician. His work focuses on photographic collage and video art, exploring immersive and multi-layered topographies through digital image alteration, sound composition, and texture-based techniques. With an academic background in biological anthropology, music, and history, he creates audiovisual narrations influenced by human anatomy and the environment.
