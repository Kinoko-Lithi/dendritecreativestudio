About the Artist

I work with the space between memory and digital matter. Fragmentation, distortion, and glitches are not errors — they are material, traces of transformation.

My practice explores the tension between clarity and collapse, structure and erosion. Each piece invites careful viewing, allowing the viewer to discover meaning within its layers, textures, and residues.

Through moving image and digital manipulation, I investigate how identity, perception, and memory shift when translated into code and pixels.
